Organize and manage your testing projects efficiently with TestSprite's Test Lists feature. Create reusable test collections, track execution history, and streamline your testing workflow.


---

## What are Test Lists?

Test Lists are organized collections of test cases that help you group related tests together. Whether you're testing different features, environments, or user flows, Test Lists provide a structured way to manage your testing efforts.

## Managing Test Lists

### <span style={{ opacity: 0.7 }}>Search and Filter</span>
